Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 3 2025

StoryStack (“we”, “our”, or “us”) provides a tool for creators and brand managers to organize, tag, sequence, and export photo stories for social media platforms. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

1. Information We Collect

A. Photos You Upload

When you upload photos into StoryStack, they are stored securely and are only accessible to your account. These photos may come from:

  • Your device’s local photo library (if you grant permission)
  • Your camera (if you take photos inside the app)
  • Cloud sources such as Dropbox, Google Drive, etc (if integrated)

B. Account Information

When you create a StoryStack account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Password (securely hashed using industry-standard encryption)

C. Usage Data

We may collect basic usage analytics such as:

  • App interactions (e.g., creating a story, exporting)
  • Device type and OS version
  • Crash logs

We DO NOT collect or store biometric data, precise location, or advertising identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only for the following purposes:

  • To allow you to upload, organize, tag, and export story sequences
  • To maintain and secure your account
  • To improve app performance and stability
  • To offer customer support

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing.

3. How Your Photos Are Stored

StoryStack stores photos that you upload or import in your account’s private storage area. Only you—and services you explicitly connect (e.g., Dropbox)—have access to your photos.

When exporting a story, we create a temporary sequence album on your device to enable seamless Instagram posting. Exported photos remain on your device unless you delete them manually.

4. Third-Party Services

StoryStack may integrate with optional third-party services such as:

  • Supabase – for user authentication and secure backend storage
  • Dropbox, Google Drive – if you choose to import photos from connected folders
  • OpenAI / GPT-4 Vision – if you enable AI tagging features

These services operate under their own privacy policies. We never provide these services with access to your account credentials or any unnecessary personal data.

5. Children’s Privacy

StoryStack is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Data Security

We follow industry best practices to keep your information safe, including:

  • Encrypted network connections (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Hashed and salted passwords
  • Private user-specific storage buckets
  • Restricted internal access

7. Your Rights

You may request:

  • Deletion of your account and all stored photos
  • A copy of the data associated with your account
  • Correction of inaccurate information

Contact us at the email below to exercise these rights.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last Updated” date.
